How to Enter

If you are new to Student Robotics and would like to express interest in taking part in the competition, please do get in contact with us. You can also have a look at the teams that are competing this year.

Teams

Student Robotics teams generally comprise about 4-6 sixth–form and college students (ages 16-18), plus an adult team-leader (usually a teacher). If you'd like to enter a team with a different make-up, please do get in contact with us, but note that the presence of a responsible adult team-leader is required.

Typically we allocate places to teams in September, around two months before Kickstart. If you have not recruited a team before submitting your entry, this is the point where you should start doing so.
